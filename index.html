Privacy Policy for FocusTimeNow

Effective Date: August 31, 2025

Data Collection

FocusTimeNow does not collect any personal information or data from users. All time tracking data is stored locally on your device only.

Data Storage

All activity data, including time entries and categories, are stored locally on your device using iOS's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device.

No Analytics or Tracking

FocusTimeNow does not use any analytics services, tracking tools, or third-party services that collect user data.

No Data Sharing

Since no data is collected, no data is shared with any third parties.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us through the App Store.

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted here.
